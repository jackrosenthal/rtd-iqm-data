Management Consulting Services for FasTracks for the year 2010. Funds for the
amendment are contained in the 2010 RTD FasTracks Budget.

Approval of Sixth Year Contract Award to Strategic Community Partners for
the FasTracks Public Information/Involvement Consultant Contract
It is recommended by the FasTracks Monitoring Committee that the RTD

consulting work for FasTracks for the year 2010. Funds for the award are

E.

Approval of Contract Amendment to Front Range Systems Consultant (FRSC)
for FasTracks Systems Engineering Consultant Services
It is recommended by the Planning and Development Committee that the RTD
Board of Directors authorize the General Manager to approve the Contract
Amendment to Front Range Systems Consultant (FRSC), in the amount of
$908,712 bringing the total authorized contract value to $30,570,017. Funds
for the contract are contained in the 2010 Recommended Budget for FasTracks.

F.

Authorization to the General Manager for Contract Amendment for FasTracks
Environmental Contract with Walsh Environmental Scientists and Engineers,
LLC
It is recommended by the FasTracks Monitoring Committee that the Board of
Directors authorize the Interim General Manager to amend work order contract
15-DO-037 with Walsh Environmental Scientists and Engineers, LLC for an
additional $5,000,000 to a revised not to exceed amount of $8,000,000. It is
also recommended that the Board of Directors authorize the Interim General
Manager authority to amend this contract as necessary to address FasTracks
environmental remediation/clean-up issues to avoid or minimize project impacts
and schedule delays due to environmental issues. RTD will continue to report
extensively to the board including the regular monthly reports and periodic
updates to the Board committees. Major decisions will be discussed with the
Board prior to action.

G.

Authorization to the General Manager for Contract Amendment for FasTracks
Environmental Contract with LT Environmental, Inc.
It is recommended by the FasTracks Monitoring Committee that the Board of
Directors authorize the Interim General Manager to amend work order contract
15-DO-007 with LT Environmental, Inc. for an additional $5,000,000 to a revised
not to exceed amount of $8,000,000. It is also recommended that the Board of
Directors authorize the Interim General Manager authority to amend this contract
as necessary to address FasTracks environmental remediation/clean-up issues to
avoid or minimize project impacts and schedule delays due to environmental
issues. RTD will continue to report extensively to the board including the regular
monthly reports and periodic updates to the Board committees. Major decisions
will be discussed with the Board prior to action.

H.

Budget Transfer for FasTracks Communication System Upgrades
It is recommended by the FasTracks Monitoring Committee that the Board of
Directors authorize the transfer of up to $3.8 million from the FasTracks Light
Rail Maintenance Facility Expansion capital project to the FasTracks System

Improvements capital project to fund improvements to the existing light rail
communications system to accommodate West Corridor construction. Funding
for this project is included in the 2009 Amended Budget for the FasTracks Light
Rail Maintenance Facility Expansion capital project.
I.

Construction Contract Award to Rail Road Specialties Inc. (RSI) for Central
Corridor E-Clip Replacement project
It is recommended by the RTD Planning & Development Committee that the RTD
Board of Directors authorize the General Manager, or designee, to execute all
documents necessary to award a construction contract to Rail Road Specialties
Inc. (RSI) in the amount of $468,750.00, for the Central Corridor E-Clip
Replacement Project. Funding for this contract is provided through the American
Recovery and Reinvestment Act of 2009 (ARRA), and is included in the 2009
Adopted Budget.

J.

Lease with Boulder County Railway Historical Society
It is recommended by the Planning and Development Committee that the RTD
Board authorize the Interim General Manager to enter into an agreement with the
Boulder County Railway Historical Society (BCRHS) to license all or a portion of
RTD Boulder Industrial Lead railroad right-of-way located west of I-25 and east of
US 287 for a period of ten years, with potential extensions. BCRHS will pay RTD
$5000 per year for the license to use the tracks.

K.

access-a-Cab Program Funding Increase
It is recommended by the Operations & Customer Services Committee that the
Board of Directors authorize the Interim General Manager to increase the
purchase orders for access-a-Cab service with Colorado Cab and Metro Taxi by
$132,000 and $268,000 respectively. This will result in purchase order totals
for 2009 of $432,000 for Colorado Cab and $568,000 for Metro Taxi.
