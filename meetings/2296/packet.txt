1.C.1.a

• ISO 9001:2008 is an international standard for quality
programs.
• RTD has maintained ISO 9001 certification of its Quality
Oversight Program since 2011.
• Certification requires that an accredited registrar review our
quality program manual, audit our procedures, and conduct
annual surveillances.
• Our last surveillance was concluded April 1, 2015.
• RTD uses Orion Registrars, headquartered in Arvada.
