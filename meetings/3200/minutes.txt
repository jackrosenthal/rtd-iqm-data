B. GMES/GMOC Charter
The committee reviewed the redlined charter and agreed to the revisions. Further the
committee also approved the deletion of the following redundant sentence under the

charter’s “Rules and Responsibilities” section under the portion applicable to all
committee members:
“Committee members must be present at every committee meeting.”

C. GM/CEO Employment Agreement
GM/CEO Johnson and the committee collectively agreed to proposed revisions to the
executive employment agreement regarding the extension of the 120-day strategic
planning window and the establishment of performance evaluation criteria for the
General Manager and CEO’s position.

D. Other Matters

• GMOC Going Forward
The committee discussed meeting times going forward and agreed to meet on
a recurring basis on the first Thursday of each month at 3:00 p.m.
