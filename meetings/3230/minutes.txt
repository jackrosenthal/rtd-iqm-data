Third Amendment to GM and CEO Employment

amendment.
Motion:

Director Buzek made the motion to approve the
Third Amendment to GM and CEO Employment.
Director Catlin seconded the motion.

Motion
To Amend:

Third Amendment to GM and CEO Employment

the Motion

To Amend:
