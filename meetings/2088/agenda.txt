UNANIMOUS CONSENT

A. Request for approval of RTD 2010 Annual Report to DRCOG on FasTracks
It is recommended by the FasTracks Monitoring Committee that the RTD Board of
Directors approve submittal of the RTD 2010 Annual Report on FasTracks to DRCOG.
This report summarizes the changes to the FasTracks Plan, including financing and
alignment, that have been approved by the Board in 2010. These changes are included
and referenced in the Annual Report.
B.

Amendment of Special Transit ADA Paratransit Services contract (17-DH-028) for the
Provision of “Federal Heights Call-n-Ride” Demand Response Services
It is recommended by the Operations/Customer Services Committee that the Board of
Directors authorize the General Manager or his designee, to amend the existing ADA
paratransit services contract with Special Transit (17-DH-028) for the provision of
the “Federal Heights Call-n-Ride” services. This contract amendment will be for the
duration of the existing contract. The cost shall not exceed $184,875. This cost
includes an estimate for fuel which will be treated as a pass through expense. The
service is scheduled to begin January 2, 2012 and run through the end of the current
ADA Paratransit Services contract,
December 31, 2012.

C. Amendment of MV Transit ADA paratransit Services contract (17-DH-007)
for the provision of “Belleview Call-n-Ride” Demand Response Services
It is recommended by the Operations/Customer Services Committee that the Board of
Directors authorize the General Manager or his designee, to amend the existing ADA
paratransit services contract with MV Transit (17-DH-007) for the provision of the
“Belleview Call-n-Ride” services. This contract amendment will be for the duration of
the existing contract. The cost shall not exceed $261,375. This cost includes an
estimate for fuel which will be treated as a pass through expense. The service is
scheduled to begin January 2, 2012 and run through the end of the current ADA
Paratransit Services contract, December 31, 2012.
D. 2010 Comprehensive Annual Financial Report and Report to Governance
It is recommended by the Financial Administration and Audit Committee that the
Board of Directors accept and enter into the record the District’s audited financial
reports relating to the year ended December 31, 2010 as prepared by Staff and
audited by the District's independent auditor, Bondi & Co. LLC.

E. Records Analyst and Document Control Services Contract-Cen-Tec
It is recommended by the Financial Administration and Audit Committee that the Board
of Directors authorize the General Manager, or his designee, to execute all documents
necessary to award Cen-Tec a contract to provide records analyst and document
control services, for the amount not to exceed of $300,000. The proposed action also
requests authorization to execute a budget transfer from capital project 10307 task 90
to expense budget code 01.3305.53012.0000.0000 in the amount of $180,000 and
another into 01.3307.53012.0000.0000 in the amount of $101,951. RTD policy
requires Board authorization of this transfer.
F. Booz Allen Hamilton, Business Process Work Order Contract, Increase of Contract
Value not-to-exceed
It is recommended by the Financial Administration and Audit Committee that the Board
of Directors authorizes the General Manager to execute all documents necessary to
increase the contract value not-to-exceed limit for an existing contract with Booz Allen
Hamilton for Business Process Services (Contract 10DJ001). This contract was
originally awarded in 2010 to Booz Allen Hamilton through the competitive bid process.
The contract allowed for an initial-year plus four (4) one-year options to be renewed
based on positive performance by the vendor. The proposed action increases the current
contract not-to-exceed limit of $450,000 by $1,000,000 to a new total contract not-toexceed limit of $1,450,000.

G. State Legislative Liaison Services
It is recommended by the Executive Committee that the Board of Directors authorize the
General Manager to enter into an agreement (for state legislative liaison services) with
Brandeberry~McKenna Public Affairs (BMPA) to exercise a one-year option (July 1,
2011 through June 30, 2012) to extend Contract No. 17-DX-001, at a cost of
$104,000, pursuant to the Contract Award agreement.

H. RTD Station and Facility Naming Policy
It is recommended by the Planning and Development Committee that the Board of
Directors adopt this station and facility naming policy, which updates a policy adopted by
the Board in December 2003.
