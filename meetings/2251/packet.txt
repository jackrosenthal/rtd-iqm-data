Procurement Methods
• Sealed Bid/Invitation for Bid (IFB)
– Purchases in excess of $100,000
– Must be publicly advertised
– Have a complete specification
– Adequate competition exists
– No discussion with bidders is needed
– Selection is based primarily on price

Procurement Methods
• Non-Competitive Procurement (Sole Source)
– Purchases in excess of $3,000
– An item is available from only a single source
– A public exigency or emergency will not permit a
delay resulting from competitive solicitation
– The FTA authorizes non-competitive negotiations
– Competition is inadequate after solicitation of a
number of sources
– Justification for Sole Source must be documented
