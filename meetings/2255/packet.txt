Title VI Equity Analyses
The FTA requires that RTD analyze major service changes for equity
as defined by Title VI of the Civil Rights Act of 1964 and
Environmental Justice (E.O. 12898).
2015 Major Equity Analyses
Service Plans to be evaluated for equity
and presented to the Board this year
include:
1. East Rail Line
2. I-225 Rail Line
3. Gold Rail Line
4. Northwest Rail Line
