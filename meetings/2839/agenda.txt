A.

Contract Amendment Authority for MCCi (Laserfiche Value Added Reseller)

It is recommended by the Financial Administration and Audit Committee that the Board of
Directors authorize the General Manager or his Designee to negotiate and execute all
documents necessary to amend Contract No. 17DO02, MCCi Laserfiche VAR Services and
Support, to increase the contract amount from a current total of $499,850 by $890,000
to a new total not-to-exceed amount of $1,389,850 (subject to further Board action by
budget authorization for budget years 2020 through 2022), in order to support RTD’s
operations, maintenance and project development based on Laserfiche software through
2022.
