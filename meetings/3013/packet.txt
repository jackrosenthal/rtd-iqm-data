#1

We will strive to keep our communities connected and our
riders, employees and region healthy and safe.

DRAFT COVID-19
BUDGET
REDUCTION
PRINCIPLES.

#2

We will take a balanced approach to budget reductions and do
so in such a way as to align with our mission. Accordingly,
service reductions should be minimized so as to minimize the
impact to our customers. We will strive for a well-rounded
approach by incorporating reductions in overhead, breaking
down silos and engaging partners, and seeking creative
approaches to service delivery.

(To be discussed by BOD on 7/14/20)

#3

We will strive to maximize mobility during the pandemic for
those customers who most depend upon our services to access
essential jobs and other necessities, while also keeping our
communities and activity centers appropriately connected.

Packet Pg. 16

Attachment: Board Presentation July 7 - Final (4246 : Mid-Term Financial Plan: Reimagine RTD, Facing

#4

We will support and value all of our employees.

#5

DRAFT COVID-19
BUDGET
REDUCTION
PRINCIPLES.
(To be discussed by BOD on 7/14/20)

We will use a thoughtful, responsible approach to decisionmaking for the COVID period, while planning for the mid-term
financial period and post-pandemic future, by incorporating
the best available data, expertise, and wisdom, including
through the Re-Imagine project, the accountability
committee, and stakeholders.

#6

We will take active measures to be transparent and seek
input regarding our use of CARES Act money as well as
in the decision-making process for measures we adopt to
respond to ongoing COVID financial impacts.
