B. Executive Session
Executive Session pursuant to § 24-6-402(4)(f)(I), C.R.S., for consideration of
personnel matters regarding recently selected General Manager/Chief Executive
Officer candidate Debra Johnson; § 24-6-402(4)(e)(I), C.R.S., for determining
positions relative to matters that may be subject to negotiations, developing strategy
for negotiations, and instructing negotiators regarding new General Manager/Chief
Executive Officer compensation and contract; and § 24-6-402(4)(b), C.R.S., to receive
legal advice regarding the same.
Motion: Director Walker made the motion to proceed into
executive session.
Director Buzek seconded the motion.
Vote on
the Motion: The motion passed by 4 in favor and 0 against.
Directors Guissinger, Lewis, and Menten were not
present for the vote.
Committee Chair Catlin declared the motion PASSED unanimously.
The committee then moved into executive session. Upon conclusion of the executive
session at 1:59 p.m., the committee resumed the meeting in open session.
