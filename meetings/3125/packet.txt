RECOMMENDED ACTION
Approval of Chief Executive Officer and General Manager Employment Agreement with Debra A.
Johnson
The General Manager Executive Search/Oversight & Performance Management Committee (Committee)
presents to the Board a motion to adopt the attached Resolution No. _____, Series of 2020, thereby
approving the terms of the Executive Employment Agreement with Debra A. Johnson.
BACKGROUND
In the Special Board Meeting held on August 25, 2020, the Board announced that Debra A. Johnson had
been selected to serve as RTD’s CEO and General Manager, pending successful employment contract
negotiations.
Contract negotiations have been ongoing since that time and are anticipated to be completed on or
before September 24, 2020.
DISCUSSION
In adopting the attached resolution, the Board approves the terms of the Executive Employment
Agreement (contract) with Debra Johnson to serve as RTD’s Chief Executive Officer and General
Manager. Following the adoption of the resolution, the parties will execute the contract.
Additionally, with the adoption of the resolution and execution of the contract, the Board acknowledges
that, upon an agreed upon start date, General Manager Johnson will begin her tenure and will
immediately assume all duties and responsibilities currently delegated to Interim General Manager Paul J.
Ballard according to the Board Bylaws, existing resolutions, and previous Board actions, unless otherwise
terminated by the Board of Directors.
FINANCIAL IMPACT
The financial impact related to the appointment of Debra Johnson as RTD’s Chief Executive Officer and
General Manager is contemplated in the Executive Employment Agreement.

RESOLUTION NO. ______, SERIES OF 2020
Appointment of Chief Executive Officer and General Manager and
Approval of Executive Employment Contract

WHEREAS, in the Special Board Meeting held on August 25, 2020, the Board announced
that Debra A. Johnson had been selected to serve as RTD’s CEO and General Manager,
pending successful employment contract negotiations; and
WHEREAS, RTD desires to employ Ms. Johnson as the Chief Executive Officer and General
Manager of RTD, and Ms. Johnson desires to serve as the Chief Executive Officer and
General Manager of RTD under the terms of the negotiated contract.
NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that:
1) The Board of Directors of the Regional Transportation District formally appoints Debra
A. Johnson to the position of Chief Executive Officer and General Manager for the
Regional Transportation District (hereafter General Manager).
2) The Board approves the terms of the Executive Employment Agreement (contract)
with Debra Johnson.
3) The Board acknowledges that, upon an agreed upon start date, General Manager
Johnson will begin her tenure and will immediately assume all duties and
responsibilities currently delegated to Interim General Manager Paul J. Ballard
according to the Board Bylaws, existing resolutions, and previous Board actions,
unless otherwise terminated by the Board of Directors.
Passed and adopted by the Board of Directors of the Regional Transportation District on the
29th day of September, 2020.
