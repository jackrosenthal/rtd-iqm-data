I.

State Legislative Issues – Updates – Jenifer Brandeberry


SB 08-046 (RTD Board Salary Increase) – This bill passed in the Senate
today 18/17. The committee applauded Jennifer for her efforts.



HB 08-1118 (Context Sensitive Solutions) – The Legislative Committee is in
opposition of this bill and took a formal vote to politely not support it.
MOTION: Director McMullen made motion to
politely not support bill.
SECOND: Director Christopher seconded the
motion.
VOTE ON
MOTION: The motion passed unanimously.

HB 08-1146 (RTD Reporting Requirements) – This bill was postponed

HB 08-1187 (Prohibition of Strikes against Public Employers) – This bill was

HB 08-1278 (Eminent Domain bill) – This bill will limit our ability to do
eminent domain. The Legislative Committee took an official position to
oppose this bill.
MOTION: Director Elfenbein made a motion to
oppose the bill.
SECOND: Director McMullen seconded the
motion.
VOTE ON
MOTION: The motion passed. Four in favor and one
opposed (Yamrick).
