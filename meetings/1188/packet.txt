2013 FasTracks Public Opinion Survey Results

Information

This will be an overview of the results from the 2013 FasTracks Public Opinion Survey. The Annual FasTracks
Public Opinion Survey was conducted from May 29 through June 5 with 800 respondents participating in the
phone survey. The purpose of the survey is to gather information from the public about RTD and the
FasTracks program, and gain a better understanding of public support, confidence, awareness and favorability
of RTD and the FasTracks program. The survey results have been compiled and evaluated by staff from the
survey vendor, BBC Research, who will present the outcome of the survey at the July 2 FasTracks Monitoring
Committee meeting.
