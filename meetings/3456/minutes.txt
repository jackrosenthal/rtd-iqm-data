Recommended Action
A.

Statement of Employee Appreciation

It is recommended by RTD Directors Catlin, Ruscha, and Sloan that the Board of
Directors incorporate a Statement of Employee Appreciation at appropriate monthly
Board meetings, as identified by the Board Chair.
Motion:

Director Sloan made the motion to move the
Statement of Employee Appreciation to the full
Board.
Director Catlin seconded the motion.

Motion to
Amend:

Director Sloan made the motion to amend the
Statement of Employee Appreciation by revising
sentences 3 and 4 to say:
We honor their work of delivering transit
excellence, service and value to the community,
especially demonstrated during difficult and
stressful moments. May the actions taken by the
Board of Directors demonstrate our profound
gratitude, commitment and support toward all
RTD employees.
Director Catlin seconded the motion.
