Northwest Rail Peak Service Study Budget and Contract Authorities

implement the transfer of $8,000,000 from the FasTracks Internal Savings Account (FISA)

to fund the Northwest Rail Peak Service Study (Study) and to enter into all required
contracts to complete the Study within the Board-approved project budget.
