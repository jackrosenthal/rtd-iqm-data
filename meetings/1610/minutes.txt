2007 Performance Measures – Third Quarter
Robert Merriman discussed this item with the Financial Administration Committee.
Due to the excessive and atypical snow patterns in January 2007 the January
statistics were eliminated from all performance measures (the board gave their
approval to do this).
The following is a list of notable Third Quarter Performance Measures:
Goal 1.1, the District met its goal for vehicle accident ratios, passenger accident
ratios, and operator/passenger assault ratios.
Goal 1.2, the District met its goals for percentage of preventative maintenance
inspections incurred as scheduled every 6,000 miles.
Goal 1.4, the District met its safety goal regarding the number of reportable
Light Rail/Auto Accidents per month.
Goal 3.1, the District did not meet its on time performance goals.
Goal 3.3, The District-wide goal for mileage between lost service maintenance
road calls was met. The Third Quarter average was 26,057 versus the goal of

Goal 4.2, the District met its goal for operator complaints per boarding.

Third Quarter 2007 Investment Report
Joe Smith, Senior Manager of Finance, briefed the committee on this item. The
benchmark was not met because of the significant amount of money market funds needed
in anticipation of procurement for rolling stock. As a result RTD has not been able to
invest the funds on the long end of yield curve. RTD was only able to invest on short end.
