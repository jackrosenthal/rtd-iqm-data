Staging for Eagle P-3 Public Presentation-Paramount Theatre May 18, 2010
Phil Washington informed the board that this plan was previously reviewed with
Chair Kemp.
Brian Middleton informed the board on the plan and handed out a seating chart.
There will be a drawing to determine the order of the presentations. There are two
teams, each will be given an hour to give their presentation and forty-five minutes of
Q & A. The RTD Board and elected officials have reserved seating; the rest is on a
first come first served basis. Both presentations will be recorded and made available
on the website. There will be a Special Board meeting on May 25, 2010 to receive
direct feedback from the public.

III.

Updates


CAC – Deborah Wilcox, Co-Chair of the CAC provided an update to the
board. The CAC held a special work session to discuss election timing and
as a result, forwarded the recommendation to the board. The CAC took a
tour of the West Corridor which was very educational and allowed them to
see what was going on and to understand the challenges. The CAC received
and are reviewing the full SB208 Report and anticipate preparing a letter of
support for delivery to the Board for May 18, 2010 board meeting.
As a way to engage more stakeholders, the CAC quarterly meetings will be
held around the district. CAC will reach out to the Directors in the district
where the quarterly meetings will be held.
The RTD board expressed how grateful they are for the caliber of the CAC
members.
