RTD’s Federal Funding Picture
• RTD receives about $74,100,000 in federal formula
grants each year (13% of total revenues)
• Current funding in FY2015 pays for existing
obligations
• No new federal funds has a devastating impact:
o Reduction of overall service levels by 15 percent
§ Elimination of 15 million trips currently taken on bus and rail,
100,000 trips on paratransit services primarily for elderly and
disabled

o Elimination of 400 jobs in our workforce
§ 225 bus and rail operators; 100 mechanics
§ 75 administrative staff
