Special Board Meeting
Chair Kemp called the meeting to order at 5:00 p.m.
I.

Roll Call

Fourteen (14) Board members were present for roll call. Director Tobiassen is out of the
country.
II.

Election of New Officers for the Executive Committee
Chair Kemp thanked all the Board members for a new year and turned the gavel
over to Director Martinez, Chair Pro Tem, for the nomination of Chair of the Board.
Motion:

Vote on
Motion:

Director Busck made the motion to appoint the current Chair,
Lee Kemp to the position of Chair of the Board for 2010.
Director Cohen seconded the motion.
Vote by acclimation with 14 affirmative votes.
Lee Kemp was announced 2010 Chair of the RTD Board of
Directors.

Chair Kemp then entertained motions for the following positions:
Motion:

Vote on
Motion:

Motion:

Director Christopher made the motion to appoint Director
Martinez as vice-chair (Chair pro Tem).
Director Busck seconded the motion.
Vote by acclimation with 14 affirmative votes.
Director Martinez was announced the 2010 Vice-Chair of the
RTD Board of Directors.
Director Daly made the motion to appoint Director Busck as

Chair Kemp thanked all the Board members for their confidence in him and this
Executive Committee. He stated he sees lots of challenges and opportunities in
2010 and looks forward to much success.

III.

Action Item
Motion:

Director James made the motion to accept the bank
Account Signature Authorization Agreement with the newly
approved officer signatures.
Director Cohen seconded the motion.

Vote on
Motion:

The vote passed 14 in favor and 0 against. Director
Tobiassen was absent.
Chair Kemp declared this action PASSED.

IV.

Other Matters
Director Martinez asked the Board to give consideration to changing the process for
the election of the officers. He and Director Busck are working on modifications
