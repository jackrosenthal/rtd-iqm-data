Minutes of the Special Board Meeting held Tuesday, August 23, 2011 at 5:30 p.m.

Special Board Meeting
I.

Call to Order
Chair Kemp called the meeting to order at 5:32 p.m.

II.

Roll Call
There were eleven (11) Board members present at roll call. Directors Daly, O’Boyle and
Riley were absent from this meeting.

III.

Action Items
A.
Resolution to Enter into Intergovernmental Agreement and Provide Funding for
the US 36 Managed Lanes Project
Marla Lien introduced this item. Marla Lien indicated this was a good
cooperative effort between CDOT, HPTE, US 36 Solutions and RTD. Del Walker
and Nadine Lee provided a presentation on the managed lane project.
Afterwards, Mike Cheroutes, CDOT-HPTE, expressed his appreciation to the
staff for their quick turnaround on this IGA and to the Board for their special
meeting to consider this item. He reiterated the collaborative effort between
the partners to advance transit issues in general.
Motion:

Director Tobiassen made the motion to approve the resolution to
enter into Intergovernmental Agreement and provide funding for
the US Managed Lanes Project.
Director Anderson seconded the motion.

Discussion:

Director Bagley inquired if there were any potential financial risk
to the agency. Marla Lien indicated the risks were minimal. She
also indicated we are passing this resolution for a good faith
effort for the $30M that RTD is committed. Director Bagley also
requested periodic reports back to the Board on this. Director
O’Boyle asked to see a copy of the draft IGA document.
Director Tayer commented this was a milestone for a major
project. This is especially a milestone for the cooperation
between RTD, US 36 and CDOT. This will provide substantial
benefit from the FasTracks project in the US 36 Corridor. He
