XI.

UNANIMOUS CONSENT
A. Approval of the Special Events - Colfax Crossover
It is recommended by the FasTracks Monitoring Committee that the Board of Directors
authorize the General Manager, or his designee, to execute all documents necessary to
award change orders to the construction contracts for Denver Transit Construction Group
(DTCG) and Balfour Beatty Rail, Inc. (BBRI) to construct the Colfax Crossover in the
amount of $1,700,000. Funding for this change order is included in the 2011 Adopted
Capital Budget for FasTracks in the Systems Improvements project, and will come from
the program-wide railroad contingency.
B. Recommendation for Passage of Resolution Authorizing the General Manager to Execute a
Purchase and Sale Agreement (PSA) for the South Market Street Station Property and
Facility
It is recommended by the FasTracks Monitoring Committee that the Board of Directors
pass the attached resolution authorizing the General Manager to execute a Purchase and
Sale Agreement (PSA) for the Market Street Station Property and Facility with the City and
County of Denver in the amount of $11,436,000 and transfer the sale proceeds to the
Denver Union Station Project Authority, subject to RTD’s retention of possession of the
Market Street Station property and Facility until its bus operations are transferred the
Denver Union Station intermodal facility.

C. Contract Amendment 2010-2011 Street Improvements & Shelter Installations Contract
#39DM008
It is recommended by the Planning & Development Committee that the RTD Board of
Directors authorize the General Manager or his designee to execute all documents
necessary to award a contract amendment to initiate the second year of a three-year
option contract for the annual Street Improvements & Shelter Installation contract with
Manion Construction, Inc. for $325,792.75. Funding for this contract amendment is
provided in the 2011 Adopted Capital Budget.
D. Approval of 2011 Contract Amendment for 16th Street Mall Transitway Lanes – Granite
Paver Maintenance
It is recommended by the Planning and Development Committee that the Board of
Directors authorize the General Manager, or his designee, to execute all documents
necessary to accomplish the following Mall Paver Contract:
Execute the second year contract amendment 2011 (option year one), first contract
amendment for maintenance of the Mall Transitway Lanes for January 1, 2011 through
December 31, 2011. The budget amount for this second year contract is $1,050,000.00.
The contract (IFB 30DT001) allows for the District to add one more future option year for
2012 beyond this current action.
E. Cost Sharing Agreement – City of Boulder/HOP
It is recommended by the Operations/Customer Services Committee that the Board of
Directors authorize the General Manager, or his designee, to execute all documents
necessary to enter into a Cost Sharing Agreement with the City of Boulder to provide RTD
financial support for the operation of the HOP circulator bus service in the commercial
areas of the City of Boulder, the University of Colorado at Boulder and adjacent business
and residential areas. This agreement is for calendar year 2011 and is for an amount not
to exceed $1,352,745.
F. Cost Sharing Agreements - FTA Section 5311 Funding
It is recommended by the Operations/Customer Services Committee that the Board of
Directors authorize the General Manager, or his designee, to execute all documents
necessary to enter into funding agreements with Special Transit of Boulder and the Senior’
Resource Center to provide RTD financial support for the provision of FTA Section 5311
(non-urbanized) services for Boulder County, Adams County and Jefferson County. These
Agreements are for calendar year 011 and for amounts not to exceed $325,532 for
Boulder County, $133,955 for Adams County and $99,270 for Jefferson County.
G. Vanpooling Agreement with DRCOG
It is recommended by the Operations/Customer Services Committee that the Board of
Directors authorize the General Manager, or his designee, to execute all documents
necessary to enter into an Agreement with the Denver Regional Council of Governments
(DRCOG) to provide RTD support for the expansion of the DRCOG RideArrangers vanpool
program in the Denver metropolitan area. This agreement is for calendar year 2011 and
shall be in an amount not to exceed $602,550.
H. Vanpooling Agreement with NFRMPO
It is recommended by the Operations/Customer Services Committee that the Board of
Directors authorize the General Manager, or his designee, to execute all documents
necessary to enter into an Agreement with the North Front Range Metropolitan Planning
Organization (NFRMPO) to provide RTD support for the expansion of the NFR VANGO
vanpool program in the Denver metropolitan area. This agreement is for calendar year
2011 and shall be in an amount not to exceed $477,405.

Cost Sharing Agreement- City of Englewood/Art Shuttle

J. Graphical Information Systems (GIS) Contractor
It is recommended by the Financial Administration and Audit Committee that the Board of
Directors authorize the General Manager to execute all documents necessary to award a
second year (option year 1) for the amount not to exceed of $115,000 for 1250 hours of
Graphical Information Systems (GIS) Consulting services. The contract was awarded to
Idea Integration following a competitive review of four submitted proposals and candidate
interviews. Idea Integration is a certified Environmental Systems Research Institute’s
(ESRI) Corporate Consultant. The contract is being brought to the Board because it is a
Personal Services contract per RTD Resolution No. 27, Series of 1985.
K. Oracle Maintenance Renewal
It is recommended by the Financial Administration and Audit Committee that the Board of
Directors authorize the General Manager to execute all documents necessary to award a
contract to Insight Public Sector in the amount of $1,027,769.16 for Oracle License
Update and Software Maintenance for approximately 18 months. Insight was selected
through the competitive bid process for this renewal.
L. Senior IT Oracle Database Contractor
It is recommended by the Financial Administration and Audit Committee that the Board of
Directors authorize the General Manager to execute all documents necessary to award a
second and final option year, for the amount not to exceed of $200,000, for Senior IT
Oracle Database services. The contract (28DH024) was originally awarded January,
2009 to Sol-IT, an SBE/DBE firm, through the competitive bid process. The contract
allowed for an initial-year plus two (2) one-year options to renew based on positive
performance by the vendor. The contract is being brought to the Board because it is a
Personal Services contract per RTD Resolution No. 27, Series of 1985.
