XII.

UNANIMOUS CONSENT
A. Recommended May 2011 Services Changes
It is recommended by the Operations/Customer Services Committee that the Board of
Directors approve moving the proposed May 2011 Service Changes (Attachment A) on to
public review and comment.
B. Certification of Contribution Rate for the RTD Defined Contribution Plan-2011
It is recommended by the Financial Administration and Audit Committee that the RTD Board of
Directors certifies a 2011 contribution rate to RTD’s Salaried Employees Defined Contribution
Plan of 9% of each participant’s eligible compensation.
C. Approval of Proposed 2011 Internal Audit Schedule
It is recommended by the Financial Administration and Audit Committee that the RTD Board of
Directors approve the proposed schedule of internal audits to be performed in 2011.
D. Approval of Ciber, Inc, IT Contingency Work Order Contract Increase of Contract Not-toExceed

It is recommended by the Financial Administration and Audit Committee that the Board of
Directors authorize the General Manager to execute all documents necessary to increase the
contract value not-to-exceed limit for an existing contract with Ciber for IT Technical and
Management Support Services (Contract 15DK008). This contract was originally awarded in
2006 to Ciber through the competitive bid process. The contract allowed for an initial-year
plus four (4) one-year options to be renewed based on positive performance by the vendor.
The final option has been executed and the period of performance extended to April 24, 2011.
The proposed action increases the current contract not-to-exceed limit of $3,050,000.00 by
$300,000.00 to a new total contract not-to-exceed limit of $3,350,000.00 as well as to
extend the contract period of performance to August 31, 2011.
E. Approval of Dataman USA, IT Contingency Work Order Contract-Increase of Contract Notto-Exceed
It is recommended by the Financial Administration and Audit Committee that the Board of
Directors authorize the General Manager to execute all documents necessary to increase the
contract value not-to-exceed limit for an existing contract with Dataman USA for IT Technical
and Management Support Services $300,000 to extend the current work order contract IT
Technical and Management Support for General Software Development Life-Cycle Services
and Migration of Legacy Software (17DK001). This contract was originally awarded May 1st,
2007 to Dataman USA, LLC (a DBE/SBE company) through the competitive bid process. The
contract allowed for an initial year plus four one year options to renew based on positive
performance by the vendor. In order to continue the fourth and final option year additional
funding must be added to the contract not to exceed. The proposed action increases the
current contract not-to-exceed limit of $915,000 by $300,000 to a new total contract not-toexceed limit of $1,215,000.
F. RTD-DRCOG Land Use Model Intergovernmental Agreement
It is recommended by the Planning and Development Committee that the Board of
Directors authorize the General Manager to execute all documents necessary to complete a
contract between RTD and the Denver Regional Council of Governments (DRCOG) for the
completion of the new socioeconomic modeling system of the Integrated Regional Model
(IRM) project in 2011-2013. Funding for this project is included in the 2011 Adopted
Budget through an $800,000 grant from the Federal Transit Administration (FTA), with
$167,000 local match provided by DRCOG. There would be no net cost to RTD.
G. Recommendation for Contract Amendment to Kaplan Kirsch and Rockwell for Legal
Assistance- Railroad Negotiations
It is recommended by the FasTracks Monitoring Committee that the RTD Board of Directors
authorize the General Manager to amend the current contract with Kaplan Kirsch and Rockwell
(KKR) for assistance in negotiations with the Union Pacific (UP) and BNSF Railroads by
extending the term to December 31, 2014 and authorizing additional funds up to $500,000
with $125,000 of that budgeted in 2011.
H. FasTracks Citizens Advisory Committee Appointments
It is recommended by the Nominating Committee for the FasTracks Citizens Advisory
Committee of the Regional Transportation District (RTD) that the RTD Board of Directors
reappoint four members of the Committee to an additional three-year term and appoint one
new member to the Committee to a three-year term.
I. Approval of Contract for CAD/AVL-Data and Voice Radio System Contract Award
It is recommended by the Planning and Development Committee that the Board of

Directors authorize the General Manager, or his designee, to execute all documents
necessary to award a contract for the replacement of RTD’s Computer Aided Dispatch and
Automatic Vehicle Location (CAD/AVL) System, Data Radio Replacement System and
Voice Radio Integration (Request For Proposal (RFP) No. 19FH018) to INIT Innovations in
Transportation, Inc. (INIT) of Chesapeake, VA in an amount not-to-exceed $47,520,000.
This price includes all of the elements of a 3-year deployment followed by a 6-year
warranty period.
