FasTracks Map
As the hub of RTD’s FasTracks
program, the redevelopment of
Denver Union Station is a crucial
component of one of the largest
transportation projects in North
America. The multimodal hub will
knit many different transportation
modes and six new public plazas
in one place with pedestrian and
bicycle connections.

Historic Union Station
Historic Union Station is the heart and
soul of the transit district in Denver. It
is getting rehabilitated to its historic
prominence. Upon completion,
Denver Union Station will be an
exciting destination and a major
transportation gateway to the region.
