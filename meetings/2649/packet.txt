Mobility Challenges for Denver
• Denver is expected to add 145,000 more residents and
190,000 more jobs by 2035
• Significant number of employees drive, bike, walk and ride
transit in (350,000) and out (125,000) of Denver each day
• Congestion and hours of delay on our existing road network is
expected to increase over the next 20 years
• Transit is not a first choice option for all

What is Denver Moves: Transit?
• Denver’s first local transit vision & implementation
plan for our city for the next 20 years
• Identification and prioritization of near- and long-term
local transit improvements based on community
input and analysis
• Guiding decision and identifying investment priorities
and implementation strategies to meet current and
future community mobility needs
