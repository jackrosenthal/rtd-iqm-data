Subject:

Minutes of the Special Board Meeting held on Tuesday, July 13 at 5:30 p.m. in
rooms R, T, and D.

Special Board Meeting
Chair Pro Tem Busck called the meeting to order at 5:30 p.m.
I.

Roll Call
Eleven (11) Board members were present for roll call. Director Brohl arrived after roll
call. Chair Kemp and Directors Martinez and Tobiassen were absent.

II.

Notice of Public Hearing on TEFRA (Tax Equity and Fiscal Responsibility Act)
Chair Pro Tem Busck read the notice of public hearing on the TEFRA. He then opened
it up for public comments. Seeing there were none, the public hearing closed at
approximately 5:40 p.m.

III.

Recommended Action
It is recommended by the FasTracks Monitoring Committee that the Board of Directors
approve the attached Resolution Number ___ (the “Bond Resolution”), authorizing the
issuance of Tax-Exempt Private Activity Bonds, Series 2010 (Denver Transit Partners
Eagle P3 Project).
Motion:

Director Pulliam made the motion to
approve the Bond Resolution authorizing
the issuance of Tax Exempt Private
Activity Bonds.
Director James seconded the motion.

Vote on
Motion:

The Motion passed 12 in favor and 0
against.
Chair Kemp and Directors
Martinez and Tobiassen were absent.
Chair Pro Tem Busck declared the motion
PASSED Unanimously.
