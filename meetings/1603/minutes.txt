1.

February Financial Status Report
Robert Merriman, Acting Manager of Budget and Financial discussed the following with
the committee:
February Financial Status Report
 Total monthly boardings were 6.4% higher in February 2007 than in February
2006. Revenue boardings were 13.7% higher.
 Combined Base and FasTracks sales tax receipts were -2.7% lower than the
2007 Adopted Budget forecast for the month and 6.7% higher than February
2006 actual receipts.
 Combined Base and FasTracks use tax receipts were 3.9% higher than the
2007 adopted budget forecast for the month.
 Combined Base and FasTracks sales tax receipts were -2.7% lower than the
2007 Adopted Budget YTD forecast.
 Combined Base and FasTracks use tax receipts were 2.0% higher than the
2007 Adopted Budget YTD forecast.
 Total Farebox Revenues collected were 14.4% higher in February 2007 than
they were in February 2006 which is primarily due to the increase in revenue
Ridership.
 Total farebox Revenues collected were 11.3% higher YTD February 2007 than
they were in YTD February 2006 which is primarily due to the increase in
revenue Ridership.

2.

Refunding Results – 2007A Sales Tax Revenue FasTracks Refunding Bonds
Teresa Sedmak, Manager of Debt and Investment, updated the committee on the
2007A FasTracks refunding bonds. We refunded approximately 364 million dollars
worth of bonds, with a Capital savings of 20.6 million and present value savings of
11.37 million over the life of the bonds which is November 2036.
