• CRL Associates Final Option Year
It is recommended by the Communications and Government Relations
Committee that the Board of Directors authorize the General Manager or her
designee to enter into an agreement for Local Government Liaison Services
with CRL Associates, Inc. (CRL) to exercise the second one-year option
(January 1, 2021 through December 31, 2021) to extend Contract No. 16DO-025, at a cost of $94,500. Funding for this contract is included in the
2021 budget.

• Lamar Transit Adjusted MAG for 2021
It is recommended by the Communications and Government Relations
Committee that the RTD Board of Directors revise the Minimum Annual
Guarantee [MAG] to be received by RTD from Lamar Transit. Due to impacts
of COVID 19, the Committee recommends a net revised revenue guarantee of
$1,085,030.00 for 2021.
This recommended action supports the following General Manager’s Goals:
Mission-Oriented Goal #3: Strong Financial Management
Strategic Priority #6: Move RTD Toward Stable Financial Situation
Motion:

Director Whitmore made the motion to move this
item to the full Board at the December 15, 2020
Board Meeting.
Chair Rivera-Malpiede seconded the motion.
