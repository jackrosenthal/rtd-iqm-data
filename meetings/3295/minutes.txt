A.

Second Amendment to the RTD Salaried Defined Contribution Plan

For the Board of Directors to approve an amendment to the salaried employee defined
contribution retirement plan to retain $200,000 in annual forfeitures for administrative
expenses.
B.

2023 Fiscal Policy

For the Board of Directors to approve the 2023 Fiscal Policy
C.

2023 Investment Policy

For the Board of Directors to approve the 2023 Investment Policy.
D.

January 2023 Title VI Service Equity Analysis

For the Board of Directors to adopt the Title VI Service Equity Analysis report for January
2023 service changes to comply with federal laws, regulations and guidelines related to
Title VI of the Civil Rights Act of 1964 as well as to meet the 2021-2026 Strategic Plan
priority of Community Value.
E.

January 2023 Service Change

For the Board of Directors to approve the January 2023 Service Change package as
revised and summarized in Attachment A and to authorize the General Manager and CEO
or her designee to execute contract amendments as necessary for private operators whose
service is impacted by these changes.
F.

Fourth Amendment to CEO and GM Employment Agreement

For the Board of Directors to adopt a Fourth Amendment to the CEO and GM Employment
Agreement to adjust the appraisal criteria for the January 1, 2022 through December 31,
2022 performance period for General Manager and CEO Debra A. Johnson as set forth in
the proposed amendment.
