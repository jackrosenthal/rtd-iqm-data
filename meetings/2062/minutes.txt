Subject:

Minutes of the Special Board Meeting held Tuesday, October 18, 2011 at 5:30 p.m.

Special Board Meeting
I.

Call to Order
Chair Pro Tem Tayer called the meeting to order at 7:01 p.m.

II.

Roll Call
There were eight (8) Board members present at roll call. Directors Anderson, Bagley,
Cohen and James were absent from this meeting.
Chair Kemp participated via Conference Call, and Director McMullen arrived after the
meeting began. Director Riley had submitted his letter of resignation.

III.

Motion to move to Executive Session
Prior to the motion to move into Executive Session, General Manager Washington
introduced the topic on contract negotiations that would be discussed in Executive
Session and asked Julio Rivera, ATU President, to share information with the Board.
Julio announced that the union had voted on the one year extension of the ATU/RTD
collective bargaining agreement. The contract was ratified 93% in favor and 7%
against. This was a significant vote of confidence in the relationship between the union
employees and management.
Motion:

Director Deadwyler made the motion to move to
Executive Session for the purpose to discuss C.R.S. 24-6402 (4) (e) Orientation to Contract Negotiations.
Director Malpiede seconded the motion.

The Motion Passed.
The Board adjourned into Executive Session at 7:05 p.m.
The Board adjourned from Executive Session at 7:17 p.m. to resume the Special
Board meeting.

IV.

Vote on Recommended Action: RTD/ATU Contract Negotiations
Chair Pro Tem Tayer asked for a motion to approve the RTD/ATU Contract
Negotiations.

Motion:

Discussion:

Vote on Motion:

Director Tobiassen made the motion to approve the
recommended action to authorize the General Manager to
execute an extension to the ATU/RTD Collective
Bargaining Agreement from March 1, 2012 to February
28, 2013.
Director Deadwyler seconded the motion.
Chair Kemp commented via phone. He complimented the
Union and RTD on doing a great job in arriving at this
agreement. This is a great step in the history of the RTDUnion relationships and he credits the efforts of our
General Manager Washington for this.
Director McMullen added he is one of the few who is still
around from the last time when the union went on strike.
He is ecstatic that things have turned out this well and
there will be no massive layoffs. He offered his
compliments to the union and to staff for such a fine
negotiation. He is proud of what we accomplished today.
Director O’Boyle stated his kudos for a job well done.
An electronic vote was taken with nine (9) in favor and
(0) against. Directors Anderson, Bagley, Cohen and
James were absent from this vote. Chair Kemp could not
vote via phone and Director Riley had resigned.
Chair Pro Tem Tayer declared this action PASSED
Unanimously.

V.

Other Matters
There were no other matters.

VI.

Adjourn to Operations and Customer Service
The meeting adjourned at 7:25 p.m.

Prepared by:

Paula Perdue, Executive Manager of the RTD Board of Directors
