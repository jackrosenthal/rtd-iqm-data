Executive Session
Executive Session pursuant to § 24-6-402(4)(f)(I), C.R.S., for consideration of personnel
matters regarding General Manager/Chief Executive Officer selection and candidate
finalists Debra Johnson, Adelee Le Grand, and Tina Quigley; § 24-6-402(4)(e)(I), C.R.S.,
for determining positions relative to matters that may be subject to negotiations,
