• Exercise Contract 19FK002 Options to Purchase 117 40-foot and 25 30foot Transit Buses
For the Board of Directors to authorize the General Manager and CEO to
exercise options and to amend contract 19FK002 (contract) with Gillig LLC
(Gillig) to purchase 117 40-foot and 25 30-foot transit buses, along with
associated spare parts and training, for a total price not to exceed
$70,271,993.
