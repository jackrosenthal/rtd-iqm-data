Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA) Committee
Participants:
 30 internal and external participants on committee
 24 of 30 internal and external participants attended
 Two of the attendees were referred by RTD Board of
Director Folska, Sheridan Walker and Kelly Egan
Discussed and developed purpose of ADAAC & ADA Committee Meetings:
 Serves to ensure, protect, inspire and facilitate RTD’s commitment to providing
transportation services and equal access to all passengers;
 To collaborate enterprise-wide with associations and organizations that serve people with
disabilities;
 To serve as a public conduit for external and internal individuals to express concerns, ask
questions and submit proposals. These proposals, questions and concerns will then become
the subject of discussions/topics for the ADA Committee meetings.
Action Items for next meeting:
 Develop and implement a Teleconference Call-In number for participants who are unable to
attend to ensure collaboration and participation from all members
 Committee to decide frequency of Meeting Dates, (quarterly or bi-monthly)
 Committee to draft agenda items and develop meeting template
