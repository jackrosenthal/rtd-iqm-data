Parking Pricing Technical
Assessment -- Scope
What is the relationship between parking charges
and ridership?
How likely are spillover impacts into adjacent
residential, commercial, and retail properties?
How do we address in- and out-of district patrons?
Would charges be implemented everywhere, or at
a subset of park and rides?
What are hypothetical net revenues based upon
alternative pricing structures?

Iliff Station Parking Management
BACKGROUND
•

Aurora negotiated with RTD
to consolidate planned
surface parking to a parking
structure in 2013.

•

Aurora contributed $7.8
million to RTD’s existing $2.7
million budget for the station

•

RTD agreed to Aurora taking
over the management of the
parking (including all
operations and maintenance
costs with an annual
contribution from RTD)

Packet Pg. 9

1.C.1.a

Attachment: Parking Activities 3.8.2016 PandD (2712 : Parking Activities)

Iliff Station Parking Management
PARKING MANAGEMENT PROPOSAL
•

Aurora is proposing to implement paid parking

•

Proposed daily rate is $3 ($50 monthly rate) with a maximum rate
of up to $5 per day

•

Aurora will be hiring a 3rd party contractor to manage the garage

•

Aurora will also be doing coordinated management of on-street
parking

•

Aurora’s proposal is consistent with the state legislation which
allows for 3rd parties to charge for parking at RTD facilities

•

IGAwith Aurora requires that the RTD Board approve Aurora’s
Parking Operation and Management Plan
