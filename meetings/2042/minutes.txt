Regional Transportation District

Memorandum
To:

Board of Directors/RTD Staff

From:

Paula Perdue, Executive Manager to the Board of Directors

Date:

August 8, 2012

Subject:

Minutes of the Special Board Meeting held on Tuesday, August 7, 2012 at 5:30
p.m.

Special Board Meeting
I.

Call to Order
Chair Kemp called the meeting to order at 5:30 p.m.

II.

Swearing in of New Board Member
Prior to the swearing in of Barry Gore as the new Director appointed by the Adams
County Commissioners for District K, Chair Kemp read into record the resolution of
this appointment.
General Counsel Marla Lien performed the swearing in ceremony.

III.

Roll Call
There were fourteen (14) Board members present at roll call. Director Bagley
arrived after roll call.

IV.

Other Matters
There were no other matters.

V.

Adjournment
The meeting adjourned at 5:35 p.m. to the Study Session.

Prepared by:

Paula Perdue, Executive Director of the RTD Board of Directors
