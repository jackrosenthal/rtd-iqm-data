Date:

Subject:

Minutes of the Special Board Meeting held on Tuesday, April 28, 2009 at 5:30 p.m.

Special Board Meeting

I.

Call to Order/Roll Call

Chair Kemp called the meeting to order at 5:37p.m. A roll call was taken with 14 present at
roll call. Director Brohl arrived after roll call.
II.

Motion to Move into Executive Session

Director Pulliam made the motion to move into Executive Session for the purpose to discuss
C.R.S. 24-6-402(4)(f) personnel matters and C.R.S. 24-6-402(4)(e) Contract Negotiations,
Denver Union Station.
Director Chacon seconded the motion. The motion passed.
III.
Adjourn
The meeting adjourned to Executive Session at 5:39 p.m.
The meeting adjourned from Executive Session at 7:35 p.m.

Prepared by:

Paula Perdue, Executive Manager of the RTD Board of Directors
