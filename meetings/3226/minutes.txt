• Northwest Rail Peak Service Study
For the RTD Board of Directors to authorize the RTD General Manager, or
her designee to implement the transfer of $12,000,000 from the FasTracks

Internal Savings Account (FISA) to fund the Northwest Rail Peak Service
Study and to enter into all required contracts to complete this Study within
the Board approved project budget.
Motion:

Director Guissinger made the motion to move
this item to the full Board at the July 20, 2021
Board Meeting.
Director Buzek seconded the motion.

Motion
To Amend:

Director Guissinger made a motion to amend so
that the Recommended Action would read as:
For the RTD Board of Directors to authorize the
RTD General Manager, or her designee to
implement the transfer of $8,000,000 plus a
10% contingency for a total amount not to
exceed $8,800,000 from the FasTracks Internal
Savings Account (FISA) to fund the Northwest
Rail Peak Service Study and to enter into all
required contracts to complete this Study within
the Board approved project budget.
Director Buzek seconded the motion.

Motion to
Postpone:

Director Whitmore made the motion to postpone
to a date certain, to August 10, 2021 with the
motion to amend intact.
Director Buzek seconded the motion.
