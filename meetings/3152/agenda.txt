B.

Justice, Equity, Diversity, and Inclusion (JEDI) Proposal

It is recommended by RTD Directors Lewis, Dishell, and Bouquet that the Board of
Directors (i) conducts quarterly Justice, Equity, Diversity, and Inclusion (“JEDI”)
workshops for at least two years facilitated by an outside consultant or facilitator at a cost
not to exceed $50,000 over two years, (ii) commits to soliciting JEDI feedback from
constituents during telephone townhalls, and (iii) charges GM/CEO to create a
comprehensive plan for JEDI work throughout the Agency.
