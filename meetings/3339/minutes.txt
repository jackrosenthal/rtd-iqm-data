• Innovations in Transportation, Inc. (INIT) CAD/AVL Software Support
Contract
For the Board of Directors to authorize the General Manager and CEO or her
designee to enter into a contract with Innovations in Transportation, Inc.
(INIT) for the implementation and operation of a software support contract
for the Computer Aided Dispatch/Automatic Vehicle Location (CAD/AVL)
district-wide dispatch system. The contract will include five years’ software
support, with five additional one-year options, for a total cost not to exceed
$7,500,000.
