B. Recommended Actions

• Adoption of the Pension Plan Contribution
It is recommended by the Financial Administration and Audit Committee that
the RTD Board of Directors adopt a 2021 contribution of $6.1 million to RTD’s
Salaried Employees’ Defined Benefit Pension Trust (the “DB Plan”), which
pays a specified monthly benefit at retirement. It is also recommended that
the RTD Board of Directors adopt a 2021 contribution of 7% of each
participant’s eligible compensation to the Salaried Employees’ Defined
Contribution Plan (the “DC Plan”), which contributes a specified amount to
the plan for each year of employment.
This recommended action supports the following General Manager’s Goals:
Mission-Oriented Goal #3: Strong Financial Management
Strategic Priority #6: Move RTD Toward Stable Financial Situation

• Approval of Free Rides for Election Day
It is recommended by Directors Lewis, Tisdale and Walker that the Board
approve the suspension of fare collection and the provision of free rides for
RTD patrons on all of its scheduled services for the period from 12:00 A.M.
MST until 11:59 P.M. MST on Tuesday, November 3, 2020.
This recommended action supports the following General Manager’s Goals:
Core Goal #5: Effective Communications & Community Outreach
Task Goal #1: Strengthen Transparent Public Communication/Collaboration
