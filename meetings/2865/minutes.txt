Approval of 2018 Public Transportation Agency Safety Plan (PTASP)

It is recommended by the Planning, Capital Programs and FasTracks Committee that the
Board of Directors approve the 2018 Public Transportation Agency Safety Plan (PTASP) as
required by 49 CFR Part 674 State Safety Oversight Regulations.
The recommended action proposed supports the following GM Goals:
Task Goal #3 Asset Management/State of Good Repair
