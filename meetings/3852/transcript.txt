G.

Director Expense Allocation Reductions for Fiscal Year 2026

For the Board to adopt the attached Resolution No. ______, Series of 2025, concerning a
reduction of Directors’ expense allocations in Fiscal Year 2026.
Due to an error in the packet pertaining to Item G, Director Expense Allocation Reductions
for Fiscal Year 2026, the original materials in the 10 28, 2025, Board Meeting packet did
not get included. therefore, a subsequent motion to table the item to the December 2,
Board Meeting will allow for the updated materials to be distributed accurately to reflect
that the item has been modified by the Executive Committee and was amended to reflect
that it would be the Director expense allocations reduction for the fiscal year 2026 being a
decrease to each Directors expense categories by 10%.
