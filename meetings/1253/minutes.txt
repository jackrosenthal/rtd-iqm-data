I.

Call to Order

II.

Installation of New Board Member District - O
Board Chair Anderson read the letter from the Boulder County Commissioners
indicating that on February 5, 2013, they had taken action to appoint Charles
(Chuck) L. Sisk to replace John Tayer as the RTD Director for District O.
Marla Lien, Legal Counsel, then performed the swearing in ceremony for newly
appointed Director Sisk.
