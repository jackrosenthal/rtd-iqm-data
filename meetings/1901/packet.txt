1.C.1.a

a. The Base System procures contracts for the sustainability and maintenance of the
Regional Transportaion District
i. Local and Federal dollars are applied to the Base System projects

b. Several of the base system contracts have DBE or SBE participation goals:
i. Currently 50 Base System contracts with DBE/SBe participation

ii. Currently there are 15 DBE Base System contracts
1. Eight (8) DBE firms perform the work as Primes

iii. Currently there are 35 SBE Base System contracts
1. Twenty- five (25) SBE firms perform the work as Primes

c. Contracts on Base System

i. Base System projects are professional services, construction, design and
engineering, maintenance services and transit services
1. Project details include: IT, concrete repair, Access-a-Ride services, Fixed
Bus Route services, light rail improvements, legal services

ii. DBE and SBE firms that have been contracted specialize in the areas of: IT
software upgrades/ testing/ intergration, concrete repair, electrical work,
consulting, construction, snow removal and custodial services

iii. Current Base System Contract Value is $368,209,191.92
1. Actual Prime Commitement to DBE/SBEs is $58,034,425.73

iv. Champion DBE/SBEs that have exceled on Base System:
1. Carnation Building Services- Snow Removal & Custodial Services
2. JCOR Mechanical – Construction
3. Manion Construction- Construction & Concrete Repair
4. P&H Equipment- Construction
5. Triunity- IT & Professional Services

d. Base System is a recommended pathway for small businesses to begin contracting with
RTD:
• Work directly under RTD to gain experience, knowledge on how to do
business with RTD
• Aids in building long term professional relationships with prime
contractors
• The contracts are more manageable contracts, to help understand a
companies’ capacity
