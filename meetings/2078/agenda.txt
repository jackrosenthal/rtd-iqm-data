C.

Recommendation for Planning Support Agreement with the BNSF Railway Company for
the Northwest Corridor Update
It is recommended by the FasTracks Monitoring Committee that the RTD Board of
Directors authorize the General Manager to enter into a contract with the BNSF Railway
Company (BNSF) for an estimated $650,000 for Planning Support including preliminary
engineering for the Northwest Rail (NWR) Corridor to provide a better cost estimate for
an RTD operating easement for NWR Corridor service.
It is further recommended by the FasTracks Monitoring Committee that the Board
of Directors authorize a transfer of $650,000 from the FasTracks Contingency
Reserve to the Northwest Rail project to fund this work in 2011.

D.

Voice Radio Equipment Hardware and Optimization Support Contract Award
It is recommended by the Operations/Customer Services Committee that the Board of
Directors authorize the General Manager, or his designee, to execute all documents
necessary to award a contract for the Fixed Radio Equipment hardware and
Optimization Support (Request for Proposal (RFP) No. 50DH028) to Motorola, Inc.

(Motorola) in an amount not-to-exceed $3,050,000. This price includes equipment,
optimization support and warranty. The purchase is through the Western States
Contracting Alliance (WSCA) contract.
E. Execution of an Oracle eBusiness Suite ERP Services Work Order Contract with Cedar
Crestone
It is recommended by the Financial Administration and Audit Committee that the
Board of Directors authorize the General Manager to execute all documents
necessary to award Cedar Crestone a three (3) year Work Order based contract to
provide technical and functional Oracle eBusiness Suite ERP services (Contract
Number 10FH026) with an annual not to exceed contract limit of $2,000,000.

F. Proposed 2011 Fiscal Policy Statement
It is recommended by the Financial Administration and Audit Committee that the
Board of Directors modify and update the Fiscal Policy Statement.
G. Approval of May 2011 Service Changes
It is recommended by the Operations/Customer Services Committee that the Board of
Directors approve the May 15, 2011 Service Change package as revised and
summarized on Attachment A resulting in a net annualized cost of approximately
$1,119,000, and, further, to authorize the General Manager to execute contract
amendments as necessary for private operators whose service is impacted by these
changes.
XII.

RECOMMENDED ACTION
H.

Lease of Parking to Mile High Development, LLC at University Station Update
It is recommended by the Planning and Development Committee that the Board of
Directors authorize the General Manager and/or his designee to enter into negotiations
and execute all documents necessary to enter into a lease agreement with Mile High
Development, LLC for the use of up to 50 parking spaces in the University Station
parking structure.

I.

Authorize an Inter-Governmental Agreement with Stakeholders for the Design/Build
Contract for the Union Avenue Bridge/Belleview Station Transit Project
It is recommended by the FasTracks Monitoring Committee that the Board of
Directors authorize the General Manager, or designee, to execute an InterGovernmental Agreement (IGA) with the stakeholders that include City and County of
Denver (CCD), Madre Metropolitan District No. 1 (MADRE), Southeast Public
Improvement Metro District (SPIMD), and Goldsmith Metro District (GOLDSMITH) for
participating in the design, construction, funding and staff involvement for a
Design/Build Contract that will provide the improvements of the proposed Union
Avenue Bridge/Belleview Station Transit project. Funding for this IGA is included in
the 2011 Adopted capital budget for the FasTracks Southeast Corridor projects.
