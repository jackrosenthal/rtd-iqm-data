Lease with Boulder County Railway Historical Society
It is recommended by the Planning and Development Committee that the RTD
Board authorize the Interim General Manager to enter into a no cost agreement
with the Boulder County Railway Historical Society (BCRHS) to license all or a
portion of RTD Boulder Industrial Lead railroad right-of-way located west of I-25
and east of US 287 for a period of ten years, with potential extensions.

Updates
 Alameda Station LLC
Rick Clarke introduced Tom Wooten, Project Manager from LLC to give the update.
They continue to make very good progress with Metro District. The bridge approval
process is a painfully slow one. The bridge is designed at 100%. There is now an
improved cleanup plan for the entire RTD site. CDOT is working through the process
of getting the I-25 reconstruction project completed between Broadway and 6th
Avenue.
