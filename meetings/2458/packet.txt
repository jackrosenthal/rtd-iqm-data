Two Related Projects
• Colfax Corridor Connections
– City and County of Denver led planning study
– Long-range vision for East Colfax transit
– Currently no funding for implementation

• 15L Route Improvements
– RTD led design and construction project
– Near-term implementation of stop and route
improvements (2016-2019)
Packet Pg. 14

Colfax Corridor Connections Project
• 2010: Denver completed the Colfax Streetcar
Feasibility Study
• 2011: Denver initiated a $3 million Alternatives
Analysis (AA) / environmental documentation study
– $2 million of funding from FTA
– $1 million of funding provided by Denver

• 2015: AA completed with Bus Rapid Transit (BRT)
selected as the preferred alternative
• Spring 2016: Anticipated completion of
environmental documentation
• Next Steps: Identify funding for implementation
(engineering design & construction)

Bus Rapid Transit Project
Definition

• 5 minute peak period service and enhanced stops
from Auraria to Anschutz
• Peak period bus only lane from Broadway to
Syracuse Street
Packet Pg. 16

15L Route Improvements Project
• Project goal: implement improvements that
will work for today and support future BRT
enhancements
• Short-term opportunity to improve transit on
East Colfax while working with the city
towards the long-term goal of BRT
– All improvements will be incorporated into the
BRT
– Project limits: Broadway to I-225 (35 stops)
