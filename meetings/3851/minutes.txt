2026 Legislative and Government Relations Program

For the Board of Directors to adopt the 2026 Legislative and Government Relations
Program.
Motion:

Director Nicholson made the motion to move
this item to the Full board at the October 28,
2025, Board meeting.
Director O’Keefe seconded the motion.

Motion To
Amend:

Director Nicholson made the motion to amend
this item with the following amendments to the
State section as follows:
•

RTD supports the passage of legislation
which reduces friction in accessing lowincome discount programs for public transit.

•

RTD supports the passage of legislation
which ensures reliable access to transit for
individuals fleeing domestic violence.

•

RTD supports the passage of legislation
which facilitates transit security by ensuring
unhoused riders are registered with local
transit agencies in order to receive fare-free
long-term transit access.

Director O’Keefe seconded the motion.
Director Nicholson withdrew his amendments.
Director O’Keefe withdrew his second.
