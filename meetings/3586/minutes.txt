Fifth Amendment to CEO and GM Employment Agreement

For the Board of Directors to adopt a Fifth Amendment to the Employment Agreement
with General Manager and CEO Debra A. Johnson to adjust the annual deadline by which
the Board shall adopt the Weighted Success Outcomes, Organizational Values, and Goals
and the distribution of the Compensation Increase between base salary and incentive
compensation for the upcoming performance period from December 31 to January 31, as
set forth in the proposed amendment.
