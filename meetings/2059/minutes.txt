Subject:

Minutes of the Special Board Meeting held on Tuesday, January 4, 2011 at 5:30
p.m.

Special Board Meeting
I.

Call to Order
Chair Kemp called the meeting to order at 5:33 p.m.

II.

Posting of Colors
The RTD Honor Guard made the presentation of colors.

III.

Swearing in of New Board Members
Former Board Member and Retired Administrative Law Judge, David Ruchman,
officiated over the swearing in of Board members. The following first time Board
members were sworn in: Directors Anderson, Deadwyler, Hoy and Riley. The
returning Board members for a second term were: Directors Daly and Tayer.
Director Malpiede was sworn in after winning her election following an appointment
by Mayor Hickenlooper.

IV.

Roll Call
There were fifteen (15) Board members present at roll call.

V.

Election of New Officers for the Executive Committee
Chair Kemp thanked all the Board members for a new year and turned the gavel
over to Director Bagley, current Secretary 2010, for the nomination of Chair of the
Board.
Motion:

Vote on
Motion:

Director Tayer made the motion to nominate the current Chair,
Lee Kemp to the position of Chair of the Board for 2011.
Director O’Boyle seconded the motion.
Vote by acclimation with 15 affirmative votes.
Lee Kemp was announced 2011 Chair of the RTD Board of
Directors.

Chair Kemp then entertained motions for the following positions:

Chair Kemp thanked all the Board members for their confidence in him and this
Executive Committee. He thinks the new Board members will bring lots of
experience and knowledge that will be a benefit to this Board. He stated he sees
lots of challenges and opportunities in 2011 and looks forward to much success.
