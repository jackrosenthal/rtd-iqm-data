RECOMMENDED ACTION
The Selection Committee of the Whole for the Interim General Manager (Selection Committee) presents
to the Board a motion to approve the employment agreement for the Interim General Manager (contract)
with certain ministerial changes as previously discussed and noted by Mr. Brett Painter.
BACKGROUND
The Selection Committee, convened in executive session during a Special Board Meeting held on
Tuesday, January 28, 2020, selected Paul Ballard to serve as Interim General Manager pending
successful contract negotiations.
DISCUSSION
Following the approval of the contract, the Interim General Manager will assume all duties delegated to
the General Manager according to the Board Bylaws, existing resolutions, and previous Board actions,
unless otherwise terminated by the Board of Directors and until such time as a permanent General
Manager is selected by the Board and such permanent General Manager’s employment agreement is
approved by the Board.
ALTERNATIVES
1) To approve the contract between the RTD Board of Directors and Paul Ballard.
2) To postpone the approval of a contract.

The financial impact associated with the adoption of this recommended action is as previously discussed
