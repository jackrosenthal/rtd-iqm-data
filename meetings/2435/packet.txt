2015 Annual Report Overview
• 3rd Annual Report captures the activities of Civil Rights
Division.
• 2015 Highlights of the Year:
• WIN program received grant funding from the FTA for the
Innovative Workforce Development Program
• WIN increased its total placements for 2015 by 30% over
previous years average placement rate
• ADA finalized over 50 plan and design reviews for ADA
compliance
• ADA established and implemented ADA/FTA compliance
measures; established the Title II Grievance procedure,
Reasonable Modification Policy, ADA Notification, etc.

2015 Annual Report Overview
Highlights of the Year (continued):
• To Date $941,450,122 has been committed to DBEs/SBEs on
Fastracks projects
• Currently $71,546,616.25 is committed to DBEs/SBEs on active
Base Systems contracts
• 774 firms are currently certified as SBEs
• Hosted National Transit Institute for the DBE Program Training
Certification which brought several agencies from the western
region
• Lead the Small/Disadvantaged Business Enterprise Advisory
Committee (SDBEAC) meeting. The Senior Leadership Team took
part in one forum and the City and County of Denver and CDOT
presented with RTD at the other. Attendance has significantly
increased and evolved.
