February Board Meeting Agenda Review
Presented by Chair Rivera-Malpiede.
Chair Rivera-Malpiede announced that she would be forming an ad hoc committee for
the purpose of reviewing the impact of the changes to the RTD Code of Conduct, as
amended, in order to work collaboratively and inclusively with Directors, staff, and key
stakeholders. She stated that the committee would be composed of 14 appointees,
including five Directors, General Manager Paul J. Ballard, Assistant General Managers
Ford and Meader, and several external members, including Roger Sherman from CRL
Associates, Evan Dreyer from Mayor Hancock’s office, Denver Police Chief Paul Pazen,

Britta Fisher from the Denver Department of Housing Stability, and representatives
from Department of Law and Denver Health.
Motion

Director Catlin made the motion to move the agenda for the
April 21, 2020 Board Meeting to the full Board.
Director Guissinger seconded the motion.

Chair Rivera-Malpiede declared the motion PASSED unanimously.
